About the Chiropractor

Dr. Greg La Cock —
World-class care,
locally delivered.

Dr. Greg La Cock completed his chiropractic training in the United States and has practiced across three continents — the USA, UK, and South Africa. Among his notable experiences, he served as chiropractor for Chelsea Football Club before returning to South Africa.

Today, Dr. La Cock divides his time between Tygerberg Chiropractic in Welgemoed and a practice in Table View. He specialises in musculoskeletal conditions and brings decades of evidence-based practice to every patient consultation.

Our Approach

Your journey to recovery

A structured, patient-first process that gets you results — from first consultation through to full recovery.

01

Consultation & Assessment

A thorough intake covering your history, symptoms, and lifestyle. We listen carefully before forming any conclusions.

02

Diagnosis & Examination

Physical examination including posture analysis, range of motion testing, orthopaedic and neurological assessment.

03

Treatment Plan

A personalised plan of care is explained to you clearly, including expected outcomes and the number of sessions recommended.

04

Ongoing Care

Follow-up adjustments, home exercise guidance, and progress reviews ensure long-term results and prevent recurrence.
